Important facts about Camels

    • Camels are often called the Ship of the Desert.
    • Camels have adapted very well to the Deserts.
    • Camels have special eyelids which help them to stand against sandstorms.
    • Camels can drink upto 200 bottles of water at a single time.
    • Camels can live in a desert for a Fortnight (14 days).

Important facts about Pigeons

    • Pigeons are found very commonly across The Globe.
    • Pigeons are known for their amazing navigational abilities.
    • In olden times, pigeons were used for delivering letters.
